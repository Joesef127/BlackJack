How to play?

  • BlackJack is absolutely a game of luck.
  • Click the "Start Game" button to start playing.
  • Two random cards are picked from the table.
  • If the sum of the cards picked is equal to "21", you have a BlackJack and you will receive a $20 bonus.
  • NOTE: You can't pick a new card after getting a BlackJack, hence you have to click the "Start Game" button to continue.
  • If the sum of the picked cards is less than "21", you keep picking new random cards till it adds up to "21".
  • NOTE: Picking new card costs $5
  • If the sum of the picked cards ends being higher than "21" right from the start or after picking new card, you've lost and have to try again if you still have up to $5 in your balance.
  • Once your money is exhausted, you're out of the game and you will not be able to pick a new card again, you have to restart the game.
  • NOTE: You can however try your luck by repeatedly clicking the "Start Game" button until it brings a BlackJack combo for you to receive $20 and continue.
